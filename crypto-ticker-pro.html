Crypto Ticker Pro

Description

If you are desirable to follow up the crypto market, it is better to go with Crypto Ticker Pro. It is a wonderful way to save the preferred currencies and store up the portfolio. It is an easy way to observe how your crypto-holdings are performing. It is reliable, quick as well as safe.

There are lots of features associated with Crypto Ticker Pro. Some of them include live cryptocurrency prices, Dark theme, freely check the 24H change, see at the 24H top and low, new day to day information available on every currency, chart starting from 1H to 1Y and store up the portfolio and observe the holdings.
